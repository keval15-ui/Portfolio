About Me

Hello! I'm Keval, a passionate creative designer with expertise in digital design and web development. I specialize in creating engaging user experiences that blend aesthetics with functionality.

With a keen eye for detail and a love for clean, modern design, I strive to create digital products that not only look beautiful but also provide intuitive user experiences.

Background

I hold a degree in Computer Science with a focus on UI/UX design. My journey in the creative field started as a self-taught designer, experimenting with various digital tools and platforms.

Over the years, I've worked with startups and established companies, helping them create meaningful digital presences and innovative products.

My Approach

I believe in a user-centered approach to design, where understanding the end-user's needs is paramount. My process involves thorough research, iterative prototyping, and continuous refinement based on feedback.
